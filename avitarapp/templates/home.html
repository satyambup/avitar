Team Avitar

Team AVITAR is a group of automobile enthusiasts who indigenously design and fabricate an All Terrain Vehicle each year to participate in various competitions across the nation like BAJA SAEINDIA, ATVC, etc. The team comprises of undergraduates from various engineering disciplines like mechanical, electrical and computer science coming together to achieve a common goal of delivering perfection and reach the pinnacle of glistening glory with hard work and perseverance.Team AVITAR is the first M-BAJA team of VIT Bhopal University
